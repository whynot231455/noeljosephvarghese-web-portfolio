## Design Context

### Users
Creative professionals, potential employers, and clients looking for a multi-disciplinary talent (UI/UX, Video Editing, Software Engineering).

### Brand Personality
**Dual-Identity / Metamodern / Precise**
- **Creative Mode**: Warm, editorial, imagery-led, cinematic, and emotional.
- **Developer Mode**: High-tech, terminal-style, logic-driven, and architectural.
- **Tone**: Sophisticated, deliberate, and high-performance.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Creative**: Minimalist with warm off-white (`#FFFDF7`), serif typography (Playfair Display/Cormorant), and fluid motion.
- **Developer**: Industrial brutalist / Matrix-inspired with near-black (`#0D1117`), monospace typography (JetBrains Mono/Space Mono), and rigid, mechanical motion.

### Design Principles
1. **The Toggle is the Star**: The transition between identities should be a "signature" experience, not just a color change.
2. **Atmospheric Motion**: Motion should reinforce the mode's personality (Fluid/Soft for Creative, Sharp/Mechanical for Developer).
3. **High-Stakes Hover**: Interactive elements should respond with premium, high-fidelity feedback.
4. **Performance First**: All animations must be GPU-accelerated and respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
5. **No Slop**: Avoid generic AI patterns (boring gradients, rounded cards with defaults).
